Privacy Policy

Trekta – Map Your World  ·  Last updated: June 2026

Summary: Trekta does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

1. Who We Are

Trekta is a personal mapping and journey-logging app developed by an independent developer. The app is available as a Progressive Web App (PWA) and through the Google Play Store.

2. What Data the App Handles

Trekta stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your profile name, and optionally your mobile number and email address (entered voluntarily in Settings)
  • Maps you create, including name, description, and photo
  • Places you log, including GPS coordinates, type, description, star rating, visit status, and photos
  • Routes recorded during a session, including type, colour, and GPS track points
  • App settings and preferences
  • Photos taken or selected within the app

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage and IndexedDB — standard device storage mechanisms. It never leaves your device unless you choose to export or share it.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your name, email address, mobile number, or any contact details
  • Your GPS location (location is only read by the app on your device and never transmitted)
  • Your photos or any media
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Device identifiers
  • Any data whatsoever — there are no servers, no accounts, and no data collection

4. GPS and Location Data

Trekta requests access to your device's GPS location to show your position on the map, log coordinates with places, and record routes. This location data is processed entirely on your device. It is stored in your local app data and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

You can use the app without granting location permission, but GPS-dependent features (live position on the map, logging GPS coordinates with places, route recording) will not be available.

5. Camera and Photos

Trekta may request access to your camera and photo library to let you attach photos to places and maps. Photos are stored locally on your device in IndexedDB. They are never uploaded or transmitted anywhere.

6. Exported Data

Important: When you use the Export feature to create a ZIP file or GPX file, the exported data may contain GPS coordinates and photos. If you choose to share this exported data (e.g. via WhatsApp or email), you are responsible for that sharing. Only share with people you trust.

The Share Place feature generates a standalone HTML report containing the place's description, type, status, rating, GPS coordinates, and any photos. Be aware of this before sharing reports.

If you have entered your mobile number or email in your profile, these will appear on shared place reports. This is optional and entirely under your control.

7. Third-Party Services

The app loads the following resources from third-party CDNs at startup:

  • Tailwind CSS (cdn.tailwindcss.com) — styling framework
  • Font Awesome (cdnjs.cloudflare.com) — icons
  • JSZip (cdn.jsdelivr.net) — ZIP file creation for export
  • QRCode.js (cdnjs.cloudflare.com) — QR code generation
  • jsQR (cdn.jsdelivr.net) — QR code scanning

These are standard, widely-used open-source libraries. Loading them may expose your IP address to those CDN providers, as is standard with any web request. No personal app data is shared with them.

The app does not use Google Analytics, advertising networks, or any tracking services.

8. Children's Privacy

Trekta is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on the security of your device. We recommend using a screen lock and keeping your device's operating system up to date.

If you use the Reset All Local Data function in Settings, all app data on that device is permanently deleted.

10. Your Rights

Since we hold no data about you on any server, there is nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete on your behalf. All your data is on your device and under your full control. You can delete it at any time using the Reset function in Settings, or by clearing the app's browser data.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
